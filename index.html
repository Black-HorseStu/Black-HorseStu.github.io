    TERMS OF SERVICE



    GENERAL CONSIDERATIONS

    - As stated before, I reserve the right to reject any order.

    - I won't accept reference links from sites that I might feel unsafe opening.

    - Please be as detailed with your request and bring as many references as you can, I may decline to work on something if not.

    - The same goes for the feedback, we're trying to make something that you love so be clear and upfront about what you think when I send the sketches/updates.

    - Commissioners don't have the right to mint/sell the drawing as an NFT.

    - Have in mind that budgets have a reason to be (hours spent, taxes, etc) so please, do not try to re-negotiate the conditions.


    RIGHTS OF USE

    - I reserve the right to use the commission in my portfolio or future commission sheets. We can discuss a timeframe of confidenciallity if your project needs it.

    - The commissions are for personal use, you're not allowed to resell them nor make merchandise with it. If you want commercial rights we can discuss it.

    - You can use it in the different media platforms (such as YouTube, Twitch, TikTok...).


    ARTSTYLE

    - I won't copy other artists work nor style, if you have a style in particular you'd like me to work with, please bring enough references and varied ones.

    - References for colours are appreciated too, if not stated I'll use my own colour palettes.


    SKETCHES & REVISIONS

    - First sketches don't have an additional fee.

    - Once I start to work in what will be the final piece you can request small changes and adjustments. Bigger changes will have an additional fee.

    - I'll try to give you as many updates as I can.

    - I reserve the right to decline complex changes or additions due to unclear or missing information.

    - The final version of the drawing will be upscaled to 1080x1080 or 1920x1080.


    PAYMENTS

    - I work with PayPal for transactions.

    - Payments will be in Euros or US Dollars (preferably US dollars).

    - The prices are set depending on the amount of details and work necessary so please, be as clear as you can with what you want!.

    - Payments are made upfront, for budgets bigger than 250$ we can discuss it.

    - Any last minute additions will have an additional fee.

    - Commissions are non-refundable.
